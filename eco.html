FROM EXTRACTION
TO EMPOWERMENT

Exclusive to King, Snohomish, and Pierce Counties

For too long, local commerce has been extractive. National conglomerates take up to 50% of every dollar spent in our neighborhoods and ship it far away, leaving behind noisy streets, congested air, and a thinning community. Lofte Eco is the physical internet for the Pacific Northwest. We are a circular, all-electric logistics and service loop designed to keep value exactly where it belongs: in your neighborhood.

The Silent Backbone

The foundation of Lofte Eco is our specialized fleet of Chevrolet Silverado EV WT trucks. Utilizing custom-built accessories and trailers, this 100% electric fleet accomplishes over 95% of our heavy lifting.

By leveraging "Pro Power Onboard" technology, our trucks act as mobile power stations. We run high-draw compressors, impact tools, and professional chainsaws directly from the truck battery. No gas generators. No diesel idling. Just silent, high-output performance that proves profit and purpose are the exact same thing.

Trash to Treasure

Recovery Works

The Circular Economy Hub

We do not have a waste problem; we have a logistics problem. Recovery Works is a hyper-local facility that diverts waste and turns it into premium goods. Through our secure metal porch boxes (the Lofte Vault), our "Milkman 2.0" service picks up your food waste and returns it as rich soil.

  • Retail sales of reclaimed kiln-dried firewood
  • Premium upcycled garden mulch, soil, and sand
  • Clean, dust-free equestrian bedding
Your Power, Uninterrupted

Grid Works

Infrastructure & Emergency Response

When the power goes out or a crisis hits, the community needs an always-on partner. Our Silverado EV fleet is essentially a distributed power grid. In a disaster, our trucks can back-feed power into a home or medical clinic using Vehicle-to-Home (V2H) technology.

  • Massive power aggregation trailers for blackouts
  • Drone reconnaissance and flood rescue support
