The Zero-Waste Verticals

Lofte Fresh

We partner with local chefs and kitchens to deliver fresh food without the "Cardboard Guilt" of national meal kits. Meals arrive in simple brown Kraft bags. Drivers place fresh ice packs into your porch cooler, or deliver directly into commercial Community Fridges inside partner apartment buildings.

Lofte Health

Partnering with independent local pharmacies, we bring transparent, "Cost-Plus" pricing straight to your door. We handle everything from standard daily medications to highly secure, same-day cold-chain deliveries. Get the care you need without waiting in a pharmacy line.

Lofte Local

The local refill revolution. We deliver glass containers of locally crafted soaps and household essentials directly to you, retrieving the empties for sanitization and reuse. We also bring fresh, roast-to-porch local coffee in reusable totes. We are keeping single-use plastics out of our neighborhoods entirely.

The Logistics Fleet

A precision-engineered logistics network built for neighborhood efficiency.

Fleet A: The Loop 6:00 AM to 2:00 PM

Vehicles: Ram ProMaster EVs

Mission: High-density household routes focusing on durable goods, standard pharmacy maintenance, and reverse logistics.

Reverse Logistics: Collecting recycling (batteries, plastic film) and retrieving empty containers (glass jugs, PakTech holders) on the inbound loop.

Fleet B: The Hotshot 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Vehicles: Mercedes eSprinter High Roofs

Mission: Same-day cold chain and high-security deliveries. Equipped with dual Coldtainers for temperature-sensitive cargo.

The Advantage: By operating a direct-to-patient daily loop, inventory never rests overnight, legally bypassing the need for expensive cold-storage facility permits and passing the savings to you.

Radical Transparency

Here is exactly how our Hotshot fleet executes flawless, cold-chain delivery every single afternoon.

Time Operational Phase Details
11:00 AM Pre-Cooling Solid ice packs are loaded from Hub freezers to pre-cool van Coldtainers to 35°F.
12:00 PM Inbound Cross-Dock Meal partners arrive at the Hub. Kraft meal bags are loaded directly into pre-chilled Coldtainers.
12:45 PM Launch Vans unplug from shore power. Coldtainer autonomous batteries take over. Fleet departs.
1:15 PM Pharmacy Sweep Driver arrives at partner pharmacy. Sensitive medications are secured directly into the active Coldtainers.
1:30 PM The Route Execution of stops at Porch Boxes and Community Fridges, while passively collecting IoT environmental data for the city.
9:00 PM Reset & Refreeze Vans return. Drivers remove thawed ice packs and place them into Hub freezers to reset for the next day.

Rolling Data Nodes: Giving Back to the City

Lofte Vector vehicles do more than deliver packages; they actively map the city in real-time. Every van is equipped with a passive sensor array tracking environmental metrics (like Air Quality and CO2), infrastructure health (like potholes and surface degradation), and telecom dead zones.

We package and sell this hyper-local data to private companies (like 5G providers) to help fund our logistics. However, in strict alignment with our ethos, we provide this exact same data free of charge to municipal city planners, researchers, and universities to help actively improve our community infrastructure.
