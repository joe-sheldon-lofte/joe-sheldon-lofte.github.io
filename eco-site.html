Managing a property usually means dealing with five different contractors and five different diesel trucks idling in your driveway. Site Works consolidates the heavy lifting into a single, professional, all-electric loop that treats your property with absolute respect.

Property Services

Renewal starts with responsible management. High-tech, low-impact solutions for your home.

Zero-Landfill Junk Removal

We are ending the dump mentality. Over 90% of what we collect is diverted from landfills into recycling or upcycling streams. Track your impact via our Live Diversion Dashboard, and receive an official Diversion Certification to prove your materials were handled responsibly.

Precision Deconstruction

We don't smash; we deconstruct. Using powerful electric breaking and cutting tools, we carefully dismantle sheds, decks, and interior spaces to maximize the recovery of reusable building materials for our local circular economy.

High-Tech Home Inspections

Knowledge is the foundation of home safety. We utilize high-fidelity sensors and thermal imaging to identify heat loss, hidden electrical hotspots, and structural fatigue that the naked eye misses, giving you total peace of mind.

Deep-Look Sewer Scoping

Stop catastrophes beneath the surface. Our high-definition fiber-optic scopes provide a live feed and recorded report of your property's plumbing and sewer lines, identifying encroaching roots, cracks, or blockages before they cause a devastating backup.

General Contracting

Home improvements tailored for residential peace. Our teams coordinate all trades under the Lofte banner, ensuring that every contractor on your site adheres to our strict silent-work and eco-friendly protocols.

White-Glove Deep Cleaning

A healthy home requires a toxic-free clean. We provide professional deep cleaning and recurring maintenance using hospital-grade, non-toxic cleaning agents and HEPA-filtered silent vacuums. Your air will be as clean as your surfaces.
