The era of "Platform Capture" is over. At Lofte, we have removed the corporate filters, the rage-bait algorithms, and the data harvesters. The Foundation

A high-fidelity environment designed for the professional media class and independent creators alike.

Removing the Filter

No Throttling: There is no algorithm to "game." If a neighbor follows you, they see your content. No one needs to shout louder than the crowd to get noticed.

Community without the Cesspool: We engineered out the shouting match by replacing traditional comment sections with a high-integrity "Letter" system for thoughtful, private responses.

A Curated Neighborhood

Safety First: Zero tolerance for incitement to harm, doxxing, dehumanization, or sexualized content. All creators are fully vetted to protect neighborhood transparency.

Political Neutrality: We accept absolutely no PAC or politician money for platform access or promotion.

The Glass Box Policy

AI Pre-Checker: A private, zero-retention AI acts as an initial safety sieve where you can check your content for potential rule violations before publishing. No surprise takedowns.

Human-Driven Review: All editorial decisions are made by a human team, never an automated script. Every moderation action is logged in a public Transparency Ledger.

Sovereign-First Economics

We provide the infrastructure. You manage the business.

The Creator Agreement
  • 15/85 Subscription Split: Lofte retains a flat 15% platform fee to maintain security and infrastructure. You retain 85% of gross revenue from subscriptions and paywalled content.
  • 0% Music Royalty Cut: Independent musicians keep 100% of streaming royalties in exchange for an agreed-upon exclusivity window.
  • Integrated Advertising: No mandatory "pre-roll" ads. You are free to integrate your own sponsors and keep 100% of that revenue.
  • Direct Audience Data: You own your subscriber list. If you choose to leave the platform, your audience moves with you.
