Finally, a roadside service that speaks the language of the modern Pacific Northwest commuter. We are the only fleet offering specialized E-bike rescue, providing rapid battery swaps, on-site chain and tire repairs, and secure transport to a local shop if needed.

The Mobile Mechanic

Comprehensive vehicle diagnostics and repair performed in your driveway. Our mechanics arrive in silent EV workshops powered by the truck's onboard battery. If a job is too complex for the driveway, we seamlessly tow the vehicle to our central Motion Works Hub and return it when finished.

Subscription Tire Swaps

Driving on the wrong tires is a safety risk, but waiting at a tire shop is a massive time sink. We use high-torque electric tools to perform full tire mounting and balancing in your driveway without noisy gas compressors. We even store your off-season tires and swap them the moment the first frost hits.

Zero-Waste Auto Detailing

A clean vehicle is a protected investment. We provide premium, showroom-grade interior and exterior detailing without the noise or the chemical runoff. We use water-reclaiming steam technology and silent electric vacuums to leave a pristine finish right in your driveway.
