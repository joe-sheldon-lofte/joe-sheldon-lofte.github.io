Core Architecture

End-to-End Encryption

We install a unique security key on your device that encrypts traffic before it hits the network. We hold no decryption keys, store no logs, and cannot respond to subpoenas.

Identity Rotation

Your IMSI (Subscriber Identity) rotates every 24 hours. To the cell towers, you look like a completely new device every single day.

Smart Tunneling

High-bandwidth, low-risk streaming (like Netflix) is automatically split from the VPN to ensure 4K speeds without compromising secure data.
