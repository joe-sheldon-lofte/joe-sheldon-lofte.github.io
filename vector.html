The Zero-Waste Verticals

Lofte Fresh

We partner with local chefs and kitchens to deliver fresh food without the cardboard guilt of national meal kits. Meals arrive in simple Kraft bags. Drivers place fresh ice packs into your porch cooler, or deliver directly into commercial Community Fridges inside partner apartment buildings.

Lofte Health

Partnering with independent local pharmacies, we bring transparent, cost-plus pricing straight to your door. We handle everything from standard daily medications to highly secure, same-day refrigerated deliveries. Get the care you need without waiting in a pharmacy line.

Lofte Local

The local refill revolution. We deliver glass containers of locally crafted soaps and household essentials directly to you, retrieving the empties for sanitization and reuse. We also bring fresh, roast-to-porch local coffee in reusable totes. We are keeping single-use plastics out of our neighborhoods entirely.

A 100% Electric Fleet

Our entire delivery network is powered by a state-of-the-art, fully electric fleet featuring Ram ProMaster EVs and Mercedes eSprinter High Roofs.

No loud diesel engines idling in your neighborhood. No exhaust fumes outside your window. Just silent, zero-emission delivery vehicles bringing local commerce directly to your door.

Giving Back to the City

Lofte Vector vehicles do more than deliver packages; they actively help improve the neighborhood. Every van is equipped with passive sensors that map air quality and track infrastructure needs like potholes and road wear.

We provide this street-level data free of charge to municipal city planners, researchers, and universities to help them build safer, cleaner, and better-maintained communities for everyone.
