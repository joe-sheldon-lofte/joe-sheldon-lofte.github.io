The Super Hub Network

To maximize efficiency and rebuild community foot traffic, we partner with independent bookstores to act as our "Super Hubs"—the Lofte equivalent of an Amazon Locker.

Instead of paying for doorstep drops, members can route their Lofte Marketplace orders (like locally roasted coffee or refillable soaps) to their neighborhood bookstore for free pickup. This drives high-intent, recurring foot traffic directly into local shops, reinforcing them as the cultural pillars of our cities.

The Zero-Waste Verticals

Lofte Fresh

We partner with local meal providers to deliver fresh food without the "Cardboard Guilt" of national meal kits. Meals arrive in simple brown Kraft bags. Drivers place fresh ice packs into your porch cooler, or deliver directly into commercial Community Fridges inside partner apartment buildings.

Lofte Health

Partnering with independent local pharmacies for transparent, "Cost-Plus" pricing. We handle everything from next-day standard medications to same-day refrigerated cold-chain deliveries. Controlled substances are secured in a bolted steel "Cab Vault," unlocking only via geofence at the patient's door.

Lofte Local

The local refill revolution. We deliver 1-gallon glass jugs of local soaps and detergents directly to you, retrieving the empties for sanitization. We also deliver bulk reusable totes of roast-to-porch local coffee, keeping single-use plastics out of our neighborhoods entirely.

The Logistics Fleet

Two dedicated fleets working in staggered shifts for ultimate utilization.

Fleet A: The Loop 6:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Vehicles: Ram ProMaster EVs

Mission: High-density household routes focusing on durable goods, standard pharmacy maintenance, and reverse logistics.

Reverse Logistics: Collecting recycling (batteries, plastic film) and retrieving empty containers (glass jugs, PakTech holders) on the inbound loop.

Fleet B: The Hotshot 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Vehicles: Mercedes eSprinter High Roofs

Mission: Same-day cold chain and high-security deliveries. Equipped with dual Coldtainers for temperature-sensitive cargo.

The Advantage: By operating a direct-to-patient daily loop, inventory never rests overnight, legally bypassing the need for expensive cold-storage facility permits.

The Daily "Hotshot" Loop

A transparent look at how we execute 80+ secure, cold-chain stops every single afternoon.

Time Operational Phase Details
11:00 AM Pre-Cooling Solid ice packs are loaded from Hub freezers to pre-cool van Coldtainers to 35°F.
12:00 PM Inbound Cross-Dock Meal partners arrive at the Hub. Kraft meal bags are loaded directly into pre-chilled Coldtainers.
12:45 PM Launch Vans unplug from shore power. Coldtainer autonomous batteries take over. Fleet departs.
1:15 PM Pharmacy Sweep Driver arrives at partner pharmacy. Meds are secured in Coldtainers or locked in the Cab Vault.
1:30 PM The Route Execution of ~80 stops at Porch Boxes and Community Fridges, while passively collecting IoT environmental data.
9:00 PM Reset & Refreeze Vans return. Drivers remove thawed ice packs and dump them into Hub freezers to reset for the next day.

Rolling Data Nodes: The IoT Sensor Stack

Lofte Vector vehicles are not just delivery vans; they map the city in real-time. Every van is equipped with a passive sensor array tracking environmental metrics (AQI, CO2), infrastructure health (potholes, surface degradation), and telecom dead zones.

We package and sell this hyper-local data to private companies (like 5G providers) to fund our logistics. In strict alignment with our ethos, we provide this exact same data free of charge to municipal city planners, researchers, and universities to help improve community infrastructure.
