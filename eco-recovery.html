We do not have a waste problem; we have a logistics problem. Recovery Works flips the script by recapturing discarded materials and turning them into high-margin, premium goods for the neighborhood.

Circular Services

A seamless, secure retail loop that takes your "junk" and gives you back a better community.

Waste Diversion Hub

To prove that "waste" is just a resource in the wrong place, we operate a hyper-local processing center. Site Works debris and neighborhood organics are mechanically and manually sorted here, ensuring a staggering 90%+ diversion rate from local landfills.

The Organics Valet

The Milkman is back. Standard recycling fails too often, so we handle the difficult stuff. We regularly collect food waste and hard-to-recycle items (like batteries, plastics, and lightbulbs) from your secure Lofte Vault, ensuring your waste actually becomes a new resource.

On-Demand Recycling

Perfect for the "Big Purge" or business-scale recycling needs. Schedule an immediate, high-volume pickup via the Lofte app. Our EVs arrive to collect and sort your specific recycling streams for direct processing at our Diversion Hub.

Encore by Lofte (Retail)

To complete the circle, we sell the premium materials recaptured from our service loop. We deliver reclaimed firewood bundles, rich compost, upcycled packaging, and clean equestrian bedding directly back to our community in a completely touchless, carbon-neutral retail loop.
