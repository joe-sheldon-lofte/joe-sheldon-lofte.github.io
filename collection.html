The Chelan Sanctuary

We do not believe in test drives. We believe in immersion. For our most discerning clients, Lofte Collection offers an exclusive, two-day retreat designed to showcase the absolute pinnacle of luxury electric marine travel.

Up to 5 guests. Fully catered. Pure silence.

Day One

The Ascent

Your journey begins at our Woodinville Flagship Studio. Leave your luggage to us as you and up to four guests step into a Lofte-commissioned Cadillac Escalade IQ. Accompanied by our Marine Director, you will enjoy a silent, chauffeured road trip over the breathtaking Cascade Mountains, arriving at our private, lakefront lodge in Manson, Washington.

The afternoon is yours to command. Step off our private dock and experience the breathtaking sensation of flying over the water in a silent, hydrofoiling Candela. As the sun sets over Lake Chelan, return to the lodge where a private chef has prepared a bespoke, multi-course dinner paired with Washington's finest curated wines.

Day Two

The Return

Wake up to the pristine quiet of the lake. Following a gourmet, chef-prepared breakfast, take one last morning cruise or simply relax on the terrace. By mid-morning, your Escalade IQ awaits.

The journey back to reality includes a curated, high-end lunch stop nestled in the mountains before your chauffeur returns you seamlessly to the Woodinville Studio or your private aviation terminal.
